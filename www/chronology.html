Swami Vivekanand

Year Month/Date Importance
1863 12 January Birth in Calcutta.
1879   Enters Presidency College
1880   Transfers to General Assembly Institution
1881 November First meeting with Sri Ramakrishna at the age of 18.
1882-86   Association with Sri Ramakrishna (4-5 Years).
1884   Passes B.A. Examination; father dies.
1885   Sri Ramakrishna's last illness
1886 16 August Sri Ramakrishna's Mahasamadhi; later, founding of Baranogore Math
  24 December Informal sannyasa vow at Antpur
1887 January Formal Sannyasa vows at Baranagore Math.
1888   Pilgrimage in northern India; returns to Baranagore Math.
1890-93   Travels all over India as parivajaka (wandering monk).
1892 December Reaches Kanyakumari, South India.
1893 13 February First public lecture, Secunderabad.
  31 May Sails for America from Bombay: First voyage to the West.
  25 July   Lands at Vancouver, Canada.
  30 July Arrives in Chicago; discovers Parliament of Religions won't open till September, needs credentials, registration period over, and Chicago is expensive.
  13 August Takes train to Boston, meets Kate Sanborn.
  26 august Meets Prof. John Henry Wright, who gives him letter to Parliament officials and train fare back to Chicago.
  11-27 September Delivered lectures at Parliament of Religions, Chicago.
  November Begins Midwestern lecture tour.
1894 14 April Begins lectures and classes on East Coast
  16 May Speaks at Harvard University
 

26 July-13 August

 At Greenpeace religious conference
  November Founds Vedanta Society of New York.
1895 January Begins classes in New York.
  4-18 June At Camp Percy, New Hampshire.
  28 June-7August Sails from New York to Paris
  August-September Paris
  Sep-Oct-Nov Lectures in London.
  6 December Sails for New York.
1896 23 February First book, Karma-Yoga, is published in New York.
  22-25 March Speaks again at Harvard, is offered chair in Eastern Philosophy.
  15 April Returns to London.
  May-July Lectures and holds classes in London.
  28 May Meets Max Muller at Oxford.
  August-Sept   In Europe
  October-November Lectures and holds classes in London.
  30 December   Sails from Naples, Italy, for India.
1897 15 January Arrives at Colombo, ceylon.
  26 January Lands at Padban, Ramnad, India-Returns to India.
  6-15 February Madras.
  19 February Arrives at Calcutta.
  1 May   Establishes Ramakrishna Mission Association in Calcutta.
  May – December Tours northwest India. Almora, U.P., Panjab, Rajasthan.
  12 June   Earthquake damages Alambazar Math.
1898 January   Returns to Calcutta.
  13 February Math    moves to Nilambar.Mukherjee's garden-house.
  4 March Belur Math land is formally purchased.
  30 March To Darjeeling.
  3 May Returns to Calcutta to set up relief operations to deal with plague.
  11 May To Almora. Begins North India pilgrimage with Western devotees.
  2 August At Amarnath Cave.
  30 Sept-6 Oct. At Kshir Bhavani.
  18 October Returns to calcutta
  9 December Consecrates Belur Math.
1899 19 March Establishes Advaita Ashrama at Mayavati.
  20 June   Leaves India: Second voyage to the West.
  31 July   Arrives in London.
  17-18 August Sails from Glasgow to New York.
  August-November At Ridgely Manor, New State.
  7 November Returns to New York City.
  3 December   Arrives in Los Angeles.
1900 22 February Arrives in San Francisco.
  14 April Founds Vedanta Society in San Francisco.
  25 June Land is donated for Shanti Ashrama.
  June   Final Classes in New Yorks.
  26 July Leaves New York for Paris.
  3 August   Arrives in Paris for International Exposition.
  7 Spetember Speaks at the Congress of History of Religions at the International Exposition.
  24 October Begins tour of Vienna, Constantinople, Athens, Cairo
  28 October Captain Sevier dies.
  6 December Sails from Cairo to Bombay : Second Return to India.
  9 December Arrives at Belur Math.
  27 December Leaves for Mayavati.
1901 3-18 January Visits Mayavati to comfort Mrs. Sevier.
  24 January Arrives in Calcutta.
  30 January Signs Trust Deed of Belur Math.
  10 February Swami Brahmananda elected first President of Ramakrishna Math and Mission.
  March-May Pilgrimage in east Bengal and As sam.
  12 May Returns to Belur Math
1902 January-February visits Buddha Gaya and Varanasi.
  8 March Returns to Belur Math.
  4 July Mahasamadhi at Belur Math at 9:10p.m.
